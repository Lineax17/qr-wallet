# QR-Wallet

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer**: This project contains a significant amount of AI-generated code and serves as an experiment to test the capabilities of modern LLMs in software development. **This project is not production-ready.**

A simple Material You themed Android app that saves your QR codes locally on your device.

## Features

- **Local Storage**: All QR codes are stored locally on your device - no cloud sync, no data collection
- **Material You Design**: Modern UI with dynamic theming support
- **Scan & Store**: Quickly scan QR codes using your camera
- **Rename**: Give your QR codes meaningful names
- **Delete**: Remove single or multiple QR codes at once
- **Reorder**: Organize your QR codes in your preferred order
- **Fullscreen View**: Display QR codes in fullscreen for easy scanning
